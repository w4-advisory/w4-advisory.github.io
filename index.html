Flagship tool

Plexiglass

Plexiglass is an open-source toolkit for detecting and protecting against vulnerabilities in Large Language Models. It is a simple CLI that lets you test LLMs against adversarial attacks — prompt injection, jailbreaking, and more — in minutes, not sprints.

Two modes cover the workflow: llm-chat converses with a model while measuring metrics like toxicity and PII leakage; llm-scan benchmarks models against open-source adversarial datasets to surface vulnerabilities before attackers do.
